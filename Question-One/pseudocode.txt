Thought Process
Input
List of objects 

Out put
String:Assigning the  priority machine to be serviced 


Process
1.Iterate through the objects in the list 
2.Check if the personnel required id less than or equal to 4.
3.Check if the history record is less than or equal to 4
4.Check if the demand is “High”
5.If so assign the highest priority serving 
6.Check if the personnel is greater than 4 and less than 10
7.Check if the history record is greater than 4 and less than 10
8.Check if the demand is “Medium”
9.If not,assign the lowest priority  for servicing.
