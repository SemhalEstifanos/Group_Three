Thought Process
Input
List of objects 

Process
Iterate through the objects in the list 
Check if the personnel required id less than or equal to 4.
Check if the history record is less than or equal to 4
Check if the demand is “High”

Check if the history record is greater than 4 and less than 10

Assign the lowest priority  for servicing.
